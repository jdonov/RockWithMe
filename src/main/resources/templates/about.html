Welcome to RockWithMe web page!

The purpose of our site is to help you find your next rock band or create your own!

In order to promote the bands every week we choose The band of The Week - the band that has most likes.

You can register as:

  • Player - meaning you are a musician
  • Producer - meaning you want to promote and sponsor rock bands
  • Fan - just checking rock bands and players info

As a player you can register your own rock band or request to join an existing band (if you have the required skills). If you create your own rock band or join an existing one, you become a member of the band and have all rights to approve or remove other members of the band, create, update or cancel band events. You can add skills - meaning the instruments that you play and the level of playing you have achieved! You can also like any band that you want.

As a producer you can only request to join a rock bands which are searching for producers. If they approve you you become a member of the band and have all rights to approve or remove other members of the band, create, update or cancel band events. You can also like any band that you want.

As a fan You can check bands and players public profiles and like any band that you want.

We also have a special role - ADMIN. You can not register as an admin (That's why it is so special). As an admin you have some statistics about the application, you can search users and add or remove roles to user. Only other admin can add you role 'ADMIN'.

ENJOY!
